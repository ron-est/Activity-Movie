Airplane!

A man afraid to fly must ensure that a plane lands safely after the pilots become sick.

Airplane! is a parody of the disaster film genre, particularly the 1957 Paramount film Zero Hour! Its use of surreal humor and fast-paced slapstick comedy, including visual and verbal puns, gags, and obscure humor don't stop coming at the audience until the end credits. Every additional viewing gives us something new to laugh at. Leslie Nielsen gets his first comedic role and opens himself up to starring in other movies directed by the Zucker brothers such as the Naked Gun series and the Scary Movie series.
